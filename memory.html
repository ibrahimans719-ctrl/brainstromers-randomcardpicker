How to Play Memory Match:

  • Click on cards to flip them over and reveal their symbols
  • Find matching pairs of cards with the same symbol
  • When you find a match, the cards will stay face up
  • Try to find all pairs in as few moves as possible
  • Your score is based on moves, time, and matches
  • Use the Hint button if you get stuck (limited uses)
  • Choose different difficulty levels for more challenge
