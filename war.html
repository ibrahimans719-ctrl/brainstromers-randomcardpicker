How to Play War:

  • Each round, you and the computer each play one card
  • The player with the higher card wins the round and gets both cards
  • Aces are high (value 14), followed by King (13), Queen (12), Jack (11)
  • If there's a tie (War!), each player places three cards face down and one face up
  • The player with the higher face-up card wins all the cards in the war
  • The game continues until one player has all the cards
  • Try to win more rounds than the computer!
