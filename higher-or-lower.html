How to Play:

  • Guess whether the next card will be higher or lower than the current card
  • Score 10 points for each correct guess
  • Build a streak for consecutive correct guesses
  • Aces are high (value 14)
  • If cards have the same value, it counts as a correct guess
  • The game ends when you run out of cards or make a wrong guess
